Portal Capture — Documentation

Portal Capture adds screen recording and annotated screenshots to your Jira Service Management customer portal request forms. Captures are attached automatically when the request is created — including for anonymous visitors.

Installation

  1. Install Portal Capture from the Atlassian Marketplace onto your Jira Cloud site.
  2. No external accounts, servers, or configuration files are needed. The app runs entirely on Atlassian Forge inside your site.

Configuration (site admin)

  1. Go to Jira Settings → Apps → Portal Capture.
  2. Select the JSM service projects where capture should be available. Only checked projects show capture buttons on their portal forms.
  3. Optionally set the staging project used for temporary storage before a request is created (defaults to the first selected project).

How customers use it

  1. A customer opens your portal and starts filling a request form.
  2. Below the form fields they see two buttons: Record your screen and Take a screenshot.
  3. Record your screen captures up to 90 seconds of screen with the browser's built-in screen sharing. Take a screenshot grabs the current tab and opens a simple annotator (arrows, boxes, highlights) before attaching.
  4. When the customer submits the request, captures are attached to the created issue as native Jira attachments. Agents see them in the issue's Attachments section.
Anonymous visitors: capture works without an Atlassian account. Visitors confirm their email on the portal form as usual; no login, extension, or download is required.

Data security

  • Pure Forge app — no external servers, no analytics, no third-party storage.
  • Recordings and screenshots are stored only as Jira attachments inside your own Atlassian site.
  • Temporary staging data is deleted automatically within 24 hours.

See the data security & privacy statement for details.

Limits

  • Recording length: up to 90 seconds per capture, 1280px max width video.
  • Browser support: any modern desktop browser with screen-capture support (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari 16.4+).
